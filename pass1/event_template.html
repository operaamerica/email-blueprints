Stage director Christopher Alden has been acclaimed for infusing traditional operas with fresh, contemporary themes and bringing innovative concepts to some of the world’s most famous houses. Alden’s productions, which have been hailed as "provocative" and "elegant" by The New York Times, challenge opera audiences with their modernist aesthetic and rich psychological undertones. Join us for a special Conversation with this celebrated director, who will reflect on his career and share stories with OPERA America President/CEO Marc A. Scorca.

7:00 p.m.
