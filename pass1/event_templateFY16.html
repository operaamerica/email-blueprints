Emerging Artist Recital Series

Opera Columbus Cooper-Bing Vocal Competition Winner
Antonina Chehovska, Soprano

Experience one of opera’s brightest up-and-coming stars when soprano Antonina Chehovska, winner of Opera Columbus’ Cooper-Bing Vocal Competition, takes the stage at the National Opera Center.

Among her roles is Tchaikovsky’s heroine in Eugene Onegin, for which she has garnered substantial praise:
"Chehovska used her generous instrument and finely tuned dramatic sense to portray a reckless young woman and a wise and experienced aristocrat. Her Tatiana … was filled with passion and trepidation; she employed facial and body gesture along with word coloring and dynamic variety to make this long scene seem brief."
– Voce di Meche
Sunday, September 13
4:00 p.m.
The National Opera Center
330 Seventh Avenue
New York, NY 10001
