From the Opera Center

The month of September always marks a return: Students return to school, summer vacationers return to work and many of our beloved arts organizations return to the stage with new production seasons. At the Opera Center, we look forward to seeing the many familiar faces returning to us, as well!

September also marks a season of change with regard to our rental rates. Beginning September 2, the Board Room will be available for a new low fee, while other spaces, which were discounted in previous months, will return to their regular rates. During the month of September, you can also receive a $10 discount on the hourly rate in our Rehearsal Hall. Click here for a full list of rates and further details.

Whether you’re a weekly visitor or an annual renter at the Opera Center, we look forward to welcoming you back soon!

My very best,

Nicholas Roberts
Opera Center Manager
